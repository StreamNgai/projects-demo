1.0.0 RC6 (2010-10-04)

New Features:

  • New RGB color picker which allows for any color to be easily selected.
  • Specify widget locations to customize the grid layout to not go behind your widgets. See "Widget Locations" under "Display" settings.
  • Opacity can now be set on background images.
  • Initial top and bottom padding is based off of device independent pixels (DIPs). This should allow the grid to be properly aligned out-of-the-box 99% of the time.
  • HUD size is now a user-configurable option and will default to an appropriate size for the display.
  • Board will rotate and resize with device orientation.

Bug Fixes:

  • Color and number preferences now only save when you click "OK" rather than when the value was changed.
  • Delete stored preferences when resetting rather than overwriting with defaults.

1.0.0 beta 5 (2010-07-30)

New Features:

  • Endless juggerdot regeneration threshold can be adjusted.
  • Sprites for fruit finally added in all their pixelated glory.
  • Support for background images added. All images will be scaled to screen dimensions and will be center cropped (if needed).
  • Added movement modes for The Man. He can use the nearest dot or random turns as a method to determine his next movement. (An intelligent AI has been pushed to a future release. Help out here.)
  • The drawing style of the entities (TheMan, Ghosts) and the dots can be toggled between fill (solid shapes) or stroke (outlines).
  • The default mode of ghosts, chase and scatter, is now fully functional.
  • Ghost frightened length now varies depending on what level you are on. While the length will be the same as the original game, the time flashing is constant and will always be the last second of the frightened state.

1.0.0 beta 3 (2010-07-25)

New Features:

  • Tapping the top, bottom, left, or right of your screen will influence The Man's direction. If you tap a direction, he will attempt to change to that direction every time he moves until he hits a wall. If he successfully changes direction he will continue going that direction until he hits a wall. If he hit a wall while going the current direction and was unable to change to your desired direction the AI will choose a new direction and your desired direction will be cleared. This does not yet work in landscape. The directions are calculated based off of the center of your screen and not The Man's location. Even though you may be tapping to The Man's left, if it is to the right of the screen center he will go right.

Bug Fixes:

  • Ghosts, The Man, Fruit, and "Ready"/"Game Over" text now rotate when in landscape.
